## Completion notes
Implemented 2026-06-10. SVG sparkline uses `preserveAspectRatio="none"` with a viewBox of `0 0 100 40` so it fills the card width. Y axis scales to global min/max across both series (with a fixed floor of 0 to prevent misleading visual expansion when values are close). Games are sorted by `gameDate` ascending to ensure correct chronological order.
